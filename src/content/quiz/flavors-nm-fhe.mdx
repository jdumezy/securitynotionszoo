explanation: "Non-malleability forbids an adversary from producing a ciphertext whose plaintext bears any meaningful relation to the challenge plaintext. FHE schemes are built precisely to enable such transformations (addition, multiplication, function evaluation). Any useful FHE scheme is therefore inherently malleable and cannot be NM-CPA secure, which is why IND (not NM) is the standard goal for FHE."
