explanation: "In approximate FHE (like CKKS), decryption produces plaintexts carrying query-dependent noise. Adjacent hybrids $H_i$ and $H_{i+1}$ differ in one ciphertext, and this difference leaks a distinguishable noise profile. The hybrid games are no longer perfectly indistinguishable, and the triangle inequality no longer gives a useful bound. This is exactly why the multi-challenge (LOR) variants of CPAD and vCCAD are strictly stronger than the single-challenge (FTG) variants."
